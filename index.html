Steps to view HTML source of any website or what I like to call "Taking an X-Ray"

  1. Right click on the website, in this case I am looking at Apple's website
  2. Click on "Inspect Element"
  3. A shortuct to skip these first two steps is to hit ctr + shift + c for Windows users or cmd + shift + c for Mac users
  4. This will allow you to not only look at the different sources of the website, but also to look at the HTML elements of this website
